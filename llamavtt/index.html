Llama VTT

A fully custom Virtual Tabletop built in Unreal Engine for cinematic, rules-agnostic play.

What is Llama VTT?

Llama VTT is my long-term project: a virtual tabletop focused on strong visuals, smooth UX, and deep system-level control. It’s built entirely in Unreal Engine and designed to support both traditional grid-based play and more dynamic, cinematic sessions.

The goal is to give DMs and players tools that feel satisfying to use: responsive camera controls, clear top-down tokens, modular maps, and a foundation that’s moddable and expandable over time.

Core Focus Areas

  • Custom grid and map system
  • Procedural environment and tooling
  • Top-down token rendering pipeline
  • Dice & physics interactions
  • Cinematic camera and UI for streaming
